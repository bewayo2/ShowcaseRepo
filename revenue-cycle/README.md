# Revenue Cycle Management

AI-powered tools for medical billing, claim recovery, and practice financial operations.

---

## Settle
**Repo:** [bewayo2/SettleApp](https://github.com/bewayo2/SettleApp) · `Python` · `Private`  
**Contact:** bewaji@bewajihealth.com · Built by BHS

AI-powered Revenue Cycle Management platform for independent medical practices. Settle addresses the financial leakage that drains practice revenue — denied claims, underpayments, slow A/R, and administrative overhead — using AI automation end-to-end.

---

### The Problem

| Benchmark | Industry Data |
|-----------|--------------|
| Initial claim denial rate | Up to **15%** (private payers) |
| Avg. time on payer admin work | **13 hrs/week** per physician |
| Target days in A/R | **30–40 days** (AAFP benchmark) |
| Net revenue lost to underpayments | **1–3%** annually |
| Denied claims overturned on appeal | **70%** — but only after costly rework |

Most independent practices don't realise how much they're losing until they see the benchmarks.

### Features

#### AI-Powered EOB Analysis
- Reads and interprets every Explanation of Benefits document in seconds
- Catches underpayments human reviewers miss
- Supports all major payer formats
- Flags denials and discrepancies instantly

#### Automated Claim Follow-Up
- Auto-generates appeals with supporting documentation
- Tracks every claim from submission to payment
- Smart prioritisation by recovery value
- Never lets a denied or underpaid claim go uncontested

#### Real-Time Revenue Dashboard
- Live cash-flow forecasting
- Payer performance scorecards
- Aging bucket analysis at a glance
- Outstanding balances and payment trends in one view

#### Smart Patient Billing
- Automatically calculates patient responsibility after insurance
- Sends clear, patient-friendly billing statements
- Automated payment reminders
- Supports payment plans — fully HIPAA compliant

---

### How It Works

**Step 1 — Connect Your Practice**  
Import patient data and billing records in minutes. Integrates with existing workflows — no IT overhaul required.

**Step 2 — AI Analyses Every Claim**  
The AI engine reviews every EOB, cross-references contracted rates, and identifies every underpayment and denial automatically.

**Step 3 — Recover What You're Owed**  
Settle generates appeals, sends patient statements, and tracks every dollar to resolution.
